About The Movement

Building the mantle and the community.

This movement functions in two expressions:

A. Souldoctor movement (the mantle)
Carries the message, the teachings, the revelations, the media, the devotionals, the books, the content, and the global assignment.

B. Salvation family (the community)

About Soul Doctor

Evangelista Embick – Soul Doctor is a woman after God’s heart, a fire-filled teacher of the Word, and a voice of healing to a generation of women who are ready to rise. She is a devoted wife and a mother. Called to restore hearts and awaken destinies, Evangelista carries a mantle for transformation through prayer, bold truth, and prophetic teaching. Whether she’s leading global prayer, mentoring women, or creating life-changing resources, her mission is simple: to lead souls back to the feet of Jesus—whole, healed, and on fire. You can find her pouring into women through her ministry, writing, and platforms designed to equip Kingdom women to build, bless, and balance their lives for God’s glory.
